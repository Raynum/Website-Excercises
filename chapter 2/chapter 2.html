What is TF2?

Team Fortress 2 is a team-based shooter with two teams (RED and BLU) and 9 unique classes.

Released in 2007 by Valve, it's known for its humor, unique art style, and balanced gameplay that has kept players engaged for over 15 years.

Key Info

  • Released: October 10, 2007
  • Developer: Valve Corporation
  • Platforms: PC, Xbox 360, PS3
  • Game Modes: Capture the Flag, Payload, Control Points, and more

Main Characters

Scout

The fastest class in the game with double jump ability.

Uses a scattergun for close-range combat and a baseball bat for melee.

Special ability: Can temporarily become invulnerable with the "Bonk!" drink.

Quote: "BONK!"

Soldier

All-American rocket launcher specialist.

Can rocket jump to reach high places and surprise enemies.

Has a shotgun as secondary weapon and shovel for melee.

Quote: "If God had wanted you to live, He would not have created ME!"

Pyro

Mysterious firebug with a flamethrower.

Can set enemies on fire causing afterburn damage.

Has a shotgun and fire axe as secondary weapons.

Special ability: Can reflect projectiles with compressed air blast.

Quote: "Mmph mph mph!"

Heavy

The team's tank with a massive minigun.

Slow movement but highest health pool.

Uses a shotgun as secondary and fists for melee.

Special ability: Can eat a sandwich to restore health.

Quote: "I am heavy weapons guy... and THIS is my weapon."
