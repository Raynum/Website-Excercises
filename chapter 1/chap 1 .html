So what is a Flying Dutchman's burger?

The Flying Dutchman Burger is a minimalist yet iconic burger that gained fame through the fast-food chain In-N-Out Burger. It was invented as part of In-N-Out's secret menu, which has become a cult favorite among fans. The burger is famous for its simplicityâ€”it consists of just two beef patties and two slices of cheese, with no bun or extra toppings. This no-frills approach highlights the quality of the ingredients and makes it a quick, satisfying meal for those who love bold, cheesy flavors.


Ingredients:

  • 2 Beef patties
  • 2 Slices of cheese
  • 1 tea spoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning
  • 1 tea spoon of butter

Method:

  1. Season the beef patties with salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce.
  2. Heat a pan with butter and cook the patties to your preferred doneness.
  3. Add cheese slices on top of the patties and let them melt.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
